Why Walkability Matters?

It’s not just about convenience—walkability shapes how we connect with our neighborhoods and the world around us. It fosters healthier lifestyles, reduces our environmental impact, and brings the essentials of daily life closer to home. When cities prioritize people over cars, streets become places for connection, local businesses thrive, and green spaces flourish.

How Does This Work?

Enter your address to calculate a total walkability score based on six key metrics: proximity to schools, workplaces, shopping, dining, green spaces, and public transport stops. We analyze walkable streets, green areas, and access to daily essentials to give you a complete picture of how walkable your area is.
