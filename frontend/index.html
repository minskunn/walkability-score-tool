Why Walkability Matters?

It is not just nice that things are close. Walkability shapes how we connect with our neighbourhoods and the world around us. It fosters healthier lifestyles, reduces our environmental impact, and brings the essentials of daily life closer to home. By designing cities for people, streets become places for connection and green spaces flourish.
